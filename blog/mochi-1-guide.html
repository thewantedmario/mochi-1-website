How to Write Better Prompts

Writing effective prompts is the key to high-quality AI video output. Focus on clarity rather than length.

  • Describe camera movement (tracking, drone, pan)
  • Specify lighting conditions (golden hour, neon, moody)
  • Define motion speed (slow motion, smooth movement)
  • Set the overall mood (cinematic, dramatic, realistic)
